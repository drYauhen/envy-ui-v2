# Visual Concepts for Hero Section (City Management)

## Recommended Concepts

### 1. Networks and Connections ⭐ (Recommended)
**Concept:** Abstract visualization of communication and transportation networks

**Visual Elements:**
- Lines and nodes connecting various points
- Graphs and connection diagrams
- Glowing lines (tech-style)
- Central node (community) with radial connections

**Style:**
- Minimalist
- Modern, tech-oriented
- Emphasis on connections and interaction

**Where to Find/Generate:**
- **Unsplash:** search "network", "city network", "urban connections"
- **Illustration libraries:** unDraw, DrawKit (city/network themes)
- **AI Generation:** Midjourney/DALL-E prompt: "abstract city network visualization, minimalist, tech style, blue and white, connecting nodes and lines"
- **SVG:** Can create embedded SVG (example already in code)

---

### 2. Urban Infrastructure
**Concept:** Modern buildings, roads, parks - elements of urban environment

**Visual Elements:**
- Modern buildings (varying heights)
- Roads and transportation arteries
- Parks and green spaces
- Cityscape from bird's eye view

**Style:**
- Photorealistic or isometric
- Emphasis on architecture and planning
- Professional, business-like

**Where to Find/Generate:**
- **Unsplash:** "city skyline", "urban planning", "modern city"
- **Pexels:** "city infrastructure", "urban development"
- **AI Generation:** "modern city skyline, aerial view, professional photography, blue hour"

## Technical Recommendations

### Image Formats:
- **Hero Section:** 1920x1080px (Full HD) or 2560x1440px (2K)
- **Split layout media:** 800x600px or 1000x750px
- **Format:** JPG (for photos) or SVG (for illustrations)

### Optimization:
- WebP for better performance
- Lazy loading for background images
- Responsive images (different sizes for mobile/desktop)

### Color Palette:
- Use system tokens: `var(--eui-color-brand-primary-base)`
- Gradients from brand-primary to brand-primary-dark
- Overlay for text readability (dark overlay 0.4-0.6 opacity)
